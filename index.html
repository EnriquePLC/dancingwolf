Dancing Wolf refers to a low-res 3D-modeled wolf dancing to an electronic dance music (EDM) remix of "Teşkilat Türküsü" by Turkish singer Mevlütcan Kaplan, which originally appeared among Turkish internet users in the early 2020s. The animation resurfaced in mid-2023 on TikTok as an exploitable meme format, after it was paired with the German song "Komm Tanzen – Ich will Nicht." becoming the subject of parodies and ironic memes over the following months in which people tried to replicate the wolf's dance moves. $DWOLF is a tribute to this viral meme.
